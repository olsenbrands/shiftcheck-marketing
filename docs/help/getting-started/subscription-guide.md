# Understanding Your ShiftCheck Subscription

## Available Plans

### Free Trial
- **Price:** $0 for 30 days
- **Includes:**
  - 1 Restaurant
  - 3 checklists per day
  - 10 tasks per checklist
  - 1 Manager
  - 3 Shift Leads
  - Unlimited Employees

### Grow Plan
- **Price:** $99/month
- **Best for:** Single store owners
- **Includes:**
  - 1 Restaurant
  - Unlimited checklists
  - Unlimited tasks
  - 2 Managers
  - Unlimited Shift Leads
  - Unlimited Employees
  - Performance Insights
  - Shift Lead Reviews
  - Weekly email reports

### Expand Plan
- **Price:** $349/month
- **Best for:** Growing groups with multiple locations
- **Includes:**
  - 5 Restaurants
  - Everything in Grow
  - 2 Managers per store
  - Multi-store reporting

### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom pricing
- **Best for:** Large multi-unit operations
- **Includes:**
  - Multi-Unit Dashboard
  - Dedicated Onboarding Manager
  - Dedicated Success Manager
  - Custom integrations

## Billing Cycle

- Subscriptions are billed monthly
- Your billing date is the day you subscribed
- You can cancel anytime (no long-term contracts)

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Select your new plan
4. You'll be charged the prorated difference immediately

### Downgrading
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Select your new plan
4. The change takes effect at your next billing date

## Trial Period

- Free trial lasts 30 days
- No credit card required to start
- Full access to trial features
- Reminders sent at 7 days and 1 day before expiration
- Convert to paid plan anytime during trial

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards

## Invoices & Receipts

- Invoices are emailed after each payment
- Access invoice history in Account Settings > Billing
- Download PDF invoices for your records
