# Creating Your ShiftCheck Account

Welcome to ShiftCheck! This guide will walk you through creating your owner account.

## Step 1: Start Sign-Up

1. Visit [shiftcheck.app/auth/signup](https://shiftcheck.app/auth/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Continue"

## Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your inbox for an email from ShiftCheck
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder

**Tip:** The verification link expires after 24 hours. Request a new one if needed.

## Step 3: Create Your Password

1. Enter a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
2. Confirm your password
3. Click "Continue"

**Password Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters
- We recommend using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

1. Enter your first and last name
2. Enter your phone number (this will be your unique identifier)
3. Optionally, enter your business name
4. Click "Continue"

## Step 5: Set Up Your Restaurant

1. Enter your restaurant name
2. Add the restaurant address
3. Select your timezone
4. Click "Continue"

## Step 6: Choose Your Plan

Select the plan that fits your needs:

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free Trial | $0 for 30 days | Testing ShiftCheck |
| Grow | $99/month | Single store owners |
| Expand | $349/month | Multi-store operations (up to 5) |

## Step 7: Complete Payment (Paid Plans)

1. Enter your payment information
2. Review your order
3. Click "Subscribe"

## You're All Set!

After completing sign-up, you'll be redirected to the ShiftCheck app where you can:
- Create checklists for your team
- Add managers and shift leads
- Start tracking shift performance
