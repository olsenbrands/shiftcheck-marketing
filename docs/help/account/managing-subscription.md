# Managing Your Subscription

## Accessing Subscription Settings

1. Log in to your ShiftCheck account
2. Click your profile icon in the top right
3. Select "Account Settings"
4. Click "Manage Subscription"

## View Current Plan

Your current plan details show:
- Plan name and price
- Next billing date
- Payment method on file
- Number of restaurants included

## Change Your Plan

### To Upgrade:
1. Click "Change Plan"
2. Select the higher tier plan
3. Review the prorated charges
4. Confirm the upgrade

**Note:** Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged the prorated difference.

### To Downgrade:
1. Click "Change Plan"
2. Select the lower tier plan
3. Review what changes
4. Confirm the downgrade

**Note:** Downgrades take effect at your next billing date. You keep full access until then.

## Update Payment Method

1. Click "Update Payment Method"
2. Enter new card details
3. Click "Save"

Your new card will be used for future payments.

## View Billing History

1. Click "View Invoices"
2. See all past payments
3. Click any invoice to download PDF

## Cancel Subscription

1. Click "Cancel Subscription"
2. Tell us why (optional)
3. Confirm cancellation

**What happens when you cancel:**
- Access continues until your current billing period ends
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation
- You can reactivate anytime

## Reactivate After Cancellation

If you've canceled but want to return:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Account Settings
3. Click "Reactivate Subscription"
4. Select your plan
5. Enter payment details
