# Common Issues & Solutions

## Sign-Up Issues

### "Email already registered"
**Problem:** You see a message that your email is already in use.

**Solution:**
1. Try logging in instead at [shiftcheck.app/auth/login](/auth/login)
2. If you forgot your password, use "Forgot Password"
3. If you never created an account, contact support@shiftcheck.app

### Verification email not received
**Problem:** You didn't receive the verification email.

**Solutions:**
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes (emails can be delayed)
3. Click "Resend verification email"
4. Try a different email address
5. Add noreply@shiftcheck.app to your contacts

### Verification link expired
**Problem:** The link in your email doesn't work.

**Solution:**
1. Return to the sign-up page
2. Enter your email again
3. Request a new verification link
4. Check for the new email immediately

## Login Issues

### "Invalid credentials"
**Problem:** Your email or password is incorrect.

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check your email spelling
2. Make sure caps lock is off
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Try the email you used during sign-up

### Account locked
**Problem:** Too many failed login attempts.

**Solution:**
1. Wait 15 minutes
2. Try again with correct credentials
3. Use "Forgot Password" if needed
4. Contact support if still locked

## Payment Issues

### Card declined
**Problem:** Your payment was rejected.

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card number, expiration, and CVC
2. Ensure sufficient funds available
3. Check if your bank blocked the transaction
4. Try a different card

### Subscription not activating
**Problem:** Payment succeeded but subscription isn't active.

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 2-3 minutes and refresh
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check your email for confirmation
4. Contact support@shiftcheck.app

## App Access Issues

### Can't access app after sign-up
**Problem:** Sign-up completed but can't log in to app.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're at app.shiftcheck.app (not shiftcheck.app)
2. Use the email from sign-up
3. Wait 5 minutes for account to sync
4. Clear browser cache and try again

### Missing restaurant
**Problem:** Your restaurant isn't showing in the app.

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure sign-up was fully completed
2. Check you're logged into correct account
3. Contact support with your phone number

## Still Need Help?

- **Chat:** Click "Need help?" on any page
- **Email:** support@shiftcheck.app
- **Response time:** Usually within 24 hours
